# Personality Values (Beliefs)

Values are the cultural and personal beliefs that dictate a creature's emotional needs and a civilization's societal goals. In entity RAWs, they are defined using `[VALUE:TOKEN:N]` (where N is -50 to 50).

## Cultural Significance (-50 to 50)
- **-50:** Complete Anathema (The trait is despised).
- **0:** Neutral (No strong cultural opinion).
- **50:** Highly Valued (The trait is the cornerstone of the culture).
- **15+:** Often required for specific societal features (e.g., `KNOWLEDGE` for libraries, `CRAFTSMANSHIP` for guilds).
