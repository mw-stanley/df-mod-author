# Dwarf Fortress Labors (v50+)

Labors are assigned to dwarves to determine which tasks they can perform. In custom buildings, the `[BUILD_LABOR]` token specifies which labor is required to construct the building.

## Mining & Woodworking
- `MINER` (Mining)
- `WOOD_CUTTER` (Wood cutting)
- `CARPENTER` (Carpentry)
- `BOWYER` (Crossbow-making)

## Stoneworking

- `STONE_CARVER` (Stone carving)
